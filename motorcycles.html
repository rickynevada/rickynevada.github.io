Motorcycles

Background

A few years ago I picked up a 50cc scooter to avoid getting a motorcycle endorsement and ride into work. That is where it all began, the continual pursuit to go faster...


Bandit 1200s

I put the most miles on this bike than any other bike, about 8 thousand over the course of a winter (5 months). I regret getting rid of this bike.

  • Dynojet stage 3 jet kit with K&N pod filters. This thing ripped!
  • Steel braided hydraulic clutch lines and brake lines and new brake pads.
  • New chain and sprockets.
  • All new fluids and filters
  • Replaced leaking engine case gasket

GSXR 600

I've put nearly 7000 miles on this bike, typical maintenance and replacing of parts. Taken it to the track a bunch of times.

  • Upgraded brake lines, brake pads, rotors
  • Quick accel kit.
  • All new fluids, filters, plugs, compression test
  • Arashi rear sets

GSXR 1100
